About SkillSwap

Our Mission

SkillSwap is a platform that connects people who want to learn new skills with those who can teach them. We believe that everyone has something valuable to share, and learning should be accessible to everyone.

How It Works

  • Create your profile - Tell us about yourself and what skills you can offer
  • Add your skills - List skills you can teach and skills you want to learn
  • Connect with others - Browse skills and find people who match your interests
  • Exchange knowledge - Chat, agree on terms, and start learning!

Features

  • Skill exchange system
  • Real-time chat
  • User ratings and reviews
  • Bookmark favorite skills
  • Admin moderation
